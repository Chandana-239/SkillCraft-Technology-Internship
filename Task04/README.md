# Task 04 - Traffic Accident Analysis Dashboard

## Objective

Analyze road traffic accident data to identify patterns related to weather conditions, road surface conditions, accident severity, time of occurrence, and major causes of accidents.

## Tool Used

* Tableau

## Dataset

Road Traffic Accident (RTA) Dataset

## Dashboard Highlights

* Accidents by Weather Condition
* Road Surface Condition Analysis
* Accidents by Hour of Day
* Accident Severity Analysis
* Accidents by Day of Week
* Cause of Accident Analysis

## Key Insights

* Most accidents occurred under normal weather conditions.
* Dry road surfaces recorded the highest number of accidents compared to wet or snowy conditions.
* Accident frequency increased during specific hours of the day, especially during peak traffic periods.
* Slight injuries were the most common accident outcome, while fatal injuries were comparatively lower.
* Certain days of the week experienced higher accident counts than others.
* Driver-related factors such as careless driving, lane changes, and insufficient distancing contributed significantly to accidents.

## Outcome

Developed an interactive Tableau dashboard to analyze traffic accident patterns and identify key factors influencing road accidents. The dashboard provides valuable insights that can support road safety awareness and data-driven decision making.
